Accountancy & Financial Analysis
The day-to-day success of your business should be your focus, and De Jong-Read is dedicated to facilitating that. Financial and management accounting, administration, and payroll support are all available to ensure you are fully compliant.

Having detailed oversight of your costs and revenue streams, and local business trends, are crucial to any sustainable business, and we have decades of experience in helping our clients stay competitive.

Just some of the services we can provide: cloud acccounting software, monthly or quarterly bookkeeping, detailed management accounts, statutory financial reporting, and deep financial analysis and forecasting.
Audit & Assurance
We deliver high quality audit services to clients in a range of business sectors. A detailed and intelligent external audit can produce insight into your busienss that can greatly enhance strategic and financial planning.

We build close working relationships with our audit clients in order to fully understand their needs and concerns and to ensure that the annual process is as smooth and mutually beneficial as possible. We understand that clients' main focus will always be their operations, so we plan our work accordingly to cause minimum disruption.

We are firm believers in the vaule of a proactive audit approach and have seen this yield benefits for numerous clients over the years.
Tax Planning & Compliance
Tax compliance can be a great burden to small business-owners, and our tax professionals can take care of everything for you. We make sure your reporting and returns are correct and timely, and let you be free to concetrate on your business.

From simple tax returns to complex planning assignments, our people boast decades of experience in getting the results our clients deserve. Whether it's personal or corporate tax, VAT or Capital Gains Tax, R&D credits or patent box advice, we have the knowledge to put your mind at ease and find the correct solutions for you.

We always strive for a "tax-first" approach to our clients' affairs, as with shrewd and forward planning, small businesses can produce significant investment opportunities and cash flow enhancements that make a real difference.
